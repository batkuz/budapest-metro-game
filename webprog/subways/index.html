Subways of Budapest

How to play (short)

Press Start game, then:

  • Click Draw card to reveal a station card.
  • Click one station, then click another station to try to draw a segment for the current metro line.
  • You may draw at most one segment per card. You may also press Skip to draw the next card without drawing.
  • Segments must be straight (horizontal, vertical, or 45° diagonal), start from a current endpoint of this line, and end at a station that matches the card (or Deák tér / Joker).
  • Segments cannot pass through other stations, cannot cross other segments in open space, and a line cannot visit the same station twice.
  • After the 8th card in a round, the next draw starts the next line.

You can extend this with scoring, switch card, abilities, etc.
